LLM AI Agent Development

Developing conversational AI systems using EMR/OCS/CDM-based data through fine-tuning large language models to support medical diagnosis and treatment decisions.

Machine Learning Prediction Models

Creating machine learning models for diagnosis and prognosis prediction in otolaryngology and neurology to support personalized treatment planning.

💊

AI Drug Repurposing

Exploring new therapeutic options for neurological and otolaryngological disorders through AI-based research to discover novel applications for existing drugs.

🔬

Digital Healthcare

Developing patient monitoring systems using wearable devices and IoT sensors to revolutionize telemedicine and personal health management.
